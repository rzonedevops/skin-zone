# Brand Entities and Attributes

## Entity: Brand
Brands are companies that create and market beauty products and treatments.

### Attributes:
- id: Unique identifier
- name: Brand name
- description: Brand description and philosophy
- website: Official website URL
- logo_url: URL to brand logo
- product_categories: Types of products offered
- target_market: Target demographic
- price_range: General price positioning (e.g., luxury, mid-range, budget)
- country_of_origin: Country where brand originated
- year_founded: Year the brand was established
- certifications: Certifications held (e.g., cruelty-free, organic)
- parent_company: Parent company if applicable
- social_media: Social media handles
