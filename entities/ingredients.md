# Ingredients Entities and Attributes

## Entity: Ingredient
Ingredients are the fundamental components used in beauty products and treatments.

### Attributes:
- id: Unique identifier
- name: Full ingredient name
- scientific_name: Scientific/botanical name
- category: Type of ingredient (e.g., plant extract, oil, chemical compound)
- description: Detailed description of the ingredient
- benefits: List of beauty/health benefits
- properties: Physical and chemical properties
- source: Origin of the ingredient (e.g., plant, synthetic)
- image_url: URL to ingredient image
- safety_rating: Safety classification
