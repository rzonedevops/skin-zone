# Product Entities and Attributes

## Entity: Product
Products are beauty and wellness items used by consumers or professionals in treatments.

### Attributes:
- id: Unique identifier
- name: Product name
- brand_id: Associated brand identifier
- category: Product category (e.g., skincare, haircare, makeup)
- subcategory: More specific classification
- description: Detailed product description
- ingredients: List of ingredients contained
- size: Product size/volume
- price: Retail price
- image_url: URL to product image
- usage_instructions: How to use the product
- benefits: Key benefits of the product
- target_concerns: Skin/hair concerns addressed
- suitable_for: Skin/hair types suitable for
- contraindications: When product should not be used
- professional_only: Whether product is for professional use only
