# Salon Entities and Attributes

## Entity: Salon
Salons are beauty and wellness establishments offering various treatments and services.

### Attributes:
- id: Unique identifier
- name: Salon name
- address: Physical location address
- city: City where salon is located
- postal_code: Postal/ZIP code
- country: Country
- phone: Contact phone number
- email: Contact email
- website: Salon website URL
- description: Detailed description of the salon
- services: List of services/treatments offered
- brands_used: Beauty brands used in the salon
- operating_hours: Business hours
- rating: Average customer rating
- review_count: Number of customer reviews
- images: URLs to salon images
- amenities: Special features (e.g., parking, wifi)
- staff: List of therapists/staff members
- specialties: Areas of specialization
