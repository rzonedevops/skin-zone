# Therapist Entities and Attributes

## Entity: Therapist
Therapists are beauty and wellness professionals who perform treatments and services.

### Attributes:
- id: Unique identifier
- name: Therapist's full name
- salon_id: Associated salon identifier
- specialties: Areas of specialization
- qualifications: Professional qualifications and certifications
- experience_years: Years of professional experience
- bio: Professional biography
- image_url: URL to therapist's photo
- treatments_offered: List of treatments the therapist can perform
- languages: Languages spoken
- availability: Working schedule
- rating: Average customer rating
- review_count: Number of customer reviews

## Sample Therapists:
While specific therapist listings weren't provided in detail, we can infer therapist types based on the treatments and salons mentioned:

### Hair Stylists
- Specialties: Hair cutting, coloring, styling, extensions, treatments
- Qualifications: Cosmetology license, specialized training in techniques like balayage, ombre, etc.

### Estheticians
- Specialties: Facials, skin treatments, chemical peels, microdermabrasion
- Qualifications: Esthetician license, brand-specific certifications (Environ, RegimA, Dermalogica)

### Nail Technicians
- Specialties: Manicures, pedicures, gel nails, nail art
- Qualifications: Nail technician certification, specialized training in techniques

### Massage Therapists
- Specialties: Various massage techniques (Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone, etc.)
- Qualifications: Massage therapy license, specialized training in specific modalities

### Makeup Artists
- Specialties: Makeup application, bridal makeup, special occasion makeup
- Qualifications: Makeup artistry certification, brand training

### Permanent Makeup Artists
- Specialties: Microblading, permanent eyeliner, lip color
- Qualifications: Permanent makeup certification, health department approval

### Lash and Brow Specialists
- Specialties: Eyelash extensions, lash lifts, brow shaping, brow tinting
- Qualifications: Lash certification, brow artistry training

### Medical Estheticians
- Specialties: Advanced skin treatments, laser treatments, injectables
- Qualifications: Medical esthetician license, specialized medical training

### Wellness Practitioners
- Specialties: Reflexology, aromatherapy, holistic treatments
- Qualifications: Specialized certifications in respective modalities
