# Treatment Entities and Attributes

## Entity: Treatment
Treatments are beauty and wellness services offered by salons and spas.

### Attributes:
- id: Unique identifier
- name: Treatment name
- category: Category of treatment (e.g., Hair, Skin, Nails, Body)
- description: Detailed description of the treatment
- duration: Typical duration of treatment
- price_range: Price range for the treatment
- required_ingredients: Ingredients used in the treatment
- required_products: Products used in the treatment
- required_equipment: Equipment needed for the treatment
- benefits: Health/beauty benefits of the treatment
- contraindications: Conditions when treatment should not be performed
- image_url: URL to treatment image
