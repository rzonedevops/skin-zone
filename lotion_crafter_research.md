## Market Insights

### Pricing Tiers Identified
1. **Ultra-Premium Actives** ($500-800/kg): Vitamin E, THDA
2. **Premium Functional** ($50-150/kg): Sepimax Zen, Silk Amino Acids
3. **Standard Functional** ($25-65/kg): Preservatives, emulsifiers
4. **Base Materials** ($8-35/kg): Stearic acid, surfactants

### Availability Patterns
- **High Availability**: Base ingredients and standard preservatives
- **Limited Stock**: Specialty biofermented ingredients
- **Sold Out Items**: Sea Kelp Bioferment (indicating high demand for marine-derived ingredients)

### Business Model Analysis
The company operates on a professional B2B model with features that indicate sophisticated customer base including formulators and manufacturers. The multi-currency support suggests significant international business, while the formulary tools indicate they serve professional cosmetic formulators rather than casual DIY customers.

### Supply Chain Insights
The wide range of bulk sizes (1kg to 55lb) and pricing tiers suggests a well-established supply chain with multiple supplier relationships. The availability of specialty ingredients like bioferments indicates connections with innovative ingredient manufacturers, while the base ingredient pricing suggests competitive sourcing for commodity chemicals.
