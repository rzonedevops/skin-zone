# Making Cosmetics Research Findings

## Company Overview
- **Website**: https://www.makingcosmetics.com/
- **Catalog Size**: 1,000+ cosmetic ingredients
- **Certifications**: ISO/GMP certified, FDA registered facility
- **Location**: Washington, USA
- **Specialization**: Wholesale cosmetic ingredients

## Key Features
- **Quality Assurance**: All products processed in ISO/GMP certified and FDA registered repackaging facility
- **Product Range**: 
  - OTC (over-the-counter) drugs including sunscreens, antiperspirants & anti-acne agents
  - USDA organic certified ingredients
  - Custom sourcing available for ingredients not in catalog
- **Packaging**: Convenient retail sizes and large bulk sizes at discount rates

## Product Categories Observed
1. **Ingredients A-Z** - Comprehensive alphabetical listing
2. **Ingredients On Sale** - Discounted items
3. **Ingredients as Bulk Specials** - Bulk pricing options
4. **Cosmeceuticals** - Active pharmaceutical ingredients
5. **Sensitive Skin Actives** - Specialized ingredients for sensitive skin
6. **Skin Tone Actives** - Ingredients for skin tone enhancement
