## Cheerleader Mascot Integration

- **Position**: Fixed in the bottom-right corner (desktop) or bottom-center (mobile)
- **Size**: Medium (128px on desktop, 64px on mobile)
- **Animation**: Standard cheering animation
- **Contextual Behavior**:
  - Shows celebration animation when product is added to cart
  - Displays supportive animation when viewing reviews
  - Shows special animation when hovering over supply chain insights
- **Interaction**:
  - Hovering reveals product-specific tips or promotions
  - Can be clicked to show personalized product recommendations
  - Special animation plays when user favorites the product

## Supply Chain Insights Visualization

The HGNN-powered visualization shows:

1. **Ingredient Journey**:
   - Sourcing locations on a map
   - Processing facilities
   - Transportation routes
   - Final manufacturing location

2. **Sustainability Metrics**:
   - Carbon footprint
   - Water usage
   - Ethical sourcing score
   - Packaging recyclability

3. **Interactive Elements**:
   - Clickable nodes for detailed information
   - Timeline of ingredient journey
   - Comparison with industry averages

## Recommended Products Section

Recommendations are powered by:

1. **HGNN Analysis**:
   - Products with complementary ingredients
   - Products frequently purchased together
   - Products with similar benefits

2. **User Behavior**:
   - Based on browsing history
   - Based on purchase history
   - Based on similar user preferences

3. **Display Format**:
   - Product image
   - Product name
   - Brand
   - Price
   - Rating
   - Quick add to cart button

## Salons Using This Product Section

Shows salons that:

1. **Use the product in treatments**:
   - Treatment name
   - Treatment price
   - Booking availability

2. **Sell the product**:
   - Current stock level
   - In-store price
   - Special promotions

3. **Display Format**:
   - Salon image
   - Salon name
   - Location
   - Rating
   - Quick booking button
