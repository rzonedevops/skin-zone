# GlossGenius Platform Research - November 2025

## Platform Information
- **Name**: GlossGenius
- **Website**: https://glossgenius.com/
- **Type**: All-in-one salon & spa management software
- **Target**: Hair salons, spas, barbershops, estheticians, med spas, nail salons, massage & wellness

## Pricing Tiers (November 2025)

### Standard Plan
- **Monthly**: $28/month
- **Annual**: $24/month (save 14%)
- **Description**: Business essentials that make doing it yourself easy
- **Features**:
  - Booking & Scheduling (booking website, calendar, resource management, waitlist)
  - Payments (2.6% processing, POS, Buy Now Pay Later, card readers, gift cards, invoices, memberships, packages)
  - Core Operations (inventory & retail management, staff management for teams <10, time tracking, reports, expense tracking)
  - Marketing (email marketing, social media templates, review management)
  - Client Management (profiles, notifications, rebooking reminders, forms & waivers)
  - Free data transfer service

### Gold Plan (Most Popular)
- **Monthly**: $56/month
- **Annual**: $48/month (save 14%)
- **Description**: Growth tools for entrepreneurs and teams
- **Features**: All Standard features plus:
  - Text Marketing (500 text credits included)
  - Analytics
  - Goal Setting
  - Financing
  - Teams of 10+ support

### Platinum Plan
- **Monthly**: $168/month
- **Annual**: $148/month (save 12%)
- **Description**: Power features and customizations for scaling growth
- **Features**: All Gold features plus:
  - Unlimited team members
  - Advanced analytics
  - 2,500 text credits included
  - Google Marketing Analytics
