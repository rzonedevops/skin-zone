## Key Observations

### Pricing Tiers
1. **Premium Actives**: $100-$760 per kg (specialty ingredients, patented compounds)
2. **Functional Ingredients**: $15-$70 per kg (emulsifiers, preservatives, thickeners)
3. **Base Ingredients**: $8-$35 per kg (surfactants, acids, basic compounds)

### Availability Patterns
- **Sold Out Items**: Sea Kelp Bioferment, RiceMATTE Powder, Sodium PCA 50%
- **High Demand Categories**: Fermented ingredients, specialty powders, humectants
- **Consistently Available**: Base ingredients, surfactants, preservatives

### Supplier Capabilities
- **Processing Time**: 1-3 business days
- **Currency Support**: USD, INR, GBP, CAD, AUD, EUR, JPY
- **Bulk Discounts**: Wholesale pricing available (excludes reward points)
- **Special Orders**: Available for sold-out items
- **Minimum Orders**: Varies by product (typically 10kg-25kg for bulk)

### Market Insights
1. Fermented bioactive ingredients command premium prices ($50-150/kg)
2. Specialty emulsifiers and thickeners in mid-range ($60-125/kg)
3. Commodity ingredients highly competitive ($8-30/kg)
4. Supply chain constraints affecting specialty ingredients
5. Strong demand for natural and biofermented actives
