## Market Position
Lotion Crafter positions itself as a professional-grade supplier focusing on bulk quantities for serious formulators and brands. The company offers comprehensive support tools including formulary resources and calculators, indicating a commitment to technical excellence. Their multi-currency support suggests a global customer base, while the 1-3 business day processing time demonstrates operational efficiency.

The pricing structure clearly reflects quality tiers, with premium actives commanding significantly higher prices than base materials. The availability of sold-out items indicates strong demand for certain specialty ingredients, particularly biofermented and novel cosmetic powders.
