# Salon & Spa Marketplace Platform Pricing Research (Nov 2025)

**Research Date**: November 18, 2025  
**Focus**: Booking systems, management software, and marketplace platforms for salons and spas

## Major Platforms - Pricing Overview

### GlossGenius
- **Pricing Range**: $24-$150/month
- **Model**: Subscription-based
- **Target**: All-in-one salon management
- **Features**: Booking, payments, marketing, client management
- **Processing Fees**: Integrated payment processing

### Vagaro
- **Pricing**: $30/month (single provider, 1 location)
- **Additional**: $10/month per additional service provider
- **Total Range**: $25-$150/month depending on team size
- **Model**: Subscription-based
- **Features**: Booking, marketing, POS, inventory management

### StyleSeat
- **Pricing Model**: Free + commission-based
- **Processing Fees**: 2.75% - 3% per transaction
- **Client Booking Fee**: $2.35 per booking (charged to client)
- **Target**: Marketplace for beauty professionals
- **Features**: Portfolio, booking, payment processing

### Fresha
- **Basic Plan**: FREE
- **Plus Plan**: $9.95 per team member/month
- **Previous Model**: Was entirely free, now tiered
- **Processing**: Commission on bookings
- **Features**: Booking platform, marketplace exposure

### Square Appointments
- **Pricing Range**: $0-$60/month
- **Free Tier**: Available with basic features
- **Paid Plans**: Start at $29/month
- **Integration**: Square POS ecosystem
- **Processing Fees**: Standard Square rates (2.6% + 10¢)

### Mindbody
- **Pricing Range**: $129-$349/month
- **Target**: Enterprise wellness and spa software
- **Features**: Advanced scheduling, marketing, business analytics
- **Model**: Subscription with tiered features

### Boulevard
- **Pricing**: $50-$500/month
- **Model**: Subscription-based
- **Target**: High-end salons and spas
- **Features**: Client experience platform, booking, CRM

### Zenoti
- **Pricing**: $50-$500/month (custom pricing)
- **Target**: Multi-location spas and salons
- **Model**: Enterprise subscription
- **Features**: Comprehensive management suite

### DaySmart Salon
- **Pricing**: Custom (flexible pricing for small businesses)
- **Model**: Subscription-based
- **Target**: Small to medium salons
- **Features**: Scheduling, POS, inventory, marketing

### Rosy Salon Software
- **Starting Price**: $44/month
- **Features Included**: Online scheduling, appointment management, mobile access, Google integration, inventory management
- **Target**: Independent salons and small teams

### Aura Salon Software
- **Pricing**: $199/month
- **Target**: Efficiency-focused salons
- **Features**: Bookings, client communication, staff management, reporting
- **Model**: All-inclusive subscription

### Appointy
- **Free Tier**: Up to 5 users
- **Paid Plans**: Start at $23.20/month
- **Model**: Freemium with feature upgrades
- **Target**: Spa scheduling and management

### DINGG
- **Pricing Range**: $50-$500/month
- **Model**: Subscription-based
- **Features**: Comprehensive salon/spa management
- **Target**: Various business sizes

- **$2.35 client booking fee**
- Revenue model based on transaction volume

### Fresha (Hybrid)

## Key Insights for Skin Zone Hypergraph

### Network Effects
- **Marketplace platforms** (StyleSeat, Fresha) create two-sided markets connecting professionals with clients
- **Software platforms** (GlossGenius, Vagaro, Mindbody) focus on business management tools
- **Hybrid models** emerging with both marketplace and management features

### Pricing Strategies
1. **Freemium**: Attract users with free tier, upsell premium features
2. **Per-seat**: Charge per team member/service provider
3. **Tiered subscriptions**: Feature-based pricing levels
4. **Transaction-based**: Commission on bookings/payments
5. **Enterprise custom**: Negotiated pricing for large operations

### Integration Opportunities for Skin Zone
- **Ingredient suppliers** ↔ **Salon platforms**: Direct supply chain integration
- **Service providers** ↔ **Product recommendations**: AI-driven ingredient matching
- **Booking platforms** ↔ **Ingredient marketplace**: Cross-platform synergy
- **Professional networks** ↔ **Supplier networks**: B2B marketplace dynamics

## Competitive Positioning

### Market Segments
1. **Independent professionals**: StyleSeat, Fresha (low/no cost)
2. **Small salons (1-5 staff)**: Vagaro, Square, Rosy ($30-$60/month)
3. **Medium salons (5-20 staff)**: GlossGenius, Boulevard ($100-$200/month)
4. **Large/multi-location**: Mindbody, Zenoti ($200-$500+/month)

### Feature Differentiation
- **Basic**: Booking, calendar, client database
- **Standard**: + Marketing, reminders, online booking
- **Advanced**: + Inventory, reporting, multi-location
- **Enterprise**: + Analytics, integrations, custom workflows
