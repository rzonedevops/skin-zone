## Market Trends (2025)

1. **Bulk Discounts**: Wholesale suppliers offer significant bulk discounts that improve profit margins for estheticians and formulators

2. **Private Label Growth**: Increasing demand for private label ingredients to speed up product launches and reduce development costs

3. **Natural/Organic Focus**: Strong market preference for certified organic and natural ingredients (USDA, CCOF certifications)

4. **Fast Processing**: Competitive advantage for suppliers with 1-3 day processing times (e.g., Lotion Crafter)

5. **Multi-Currency Support**: Global suppliers supporting multiple currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, INR, JPY)

6. **Technical Support**: Value-added services like formulary tools, calculators, and formulation assistance

7. **Quality Certifications**: ISO, GMP, FDA registration, and organic certifications are standard expectations
