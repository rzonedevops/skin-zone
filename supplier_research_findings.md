- **Location**: Washington, USA
- **Special Features**: 
  - OTC drugs including sunscreens, antiperspirants & anti-acne agents

  - Custom sourcing available

### 2. Lotion Crafter
- **Website**: https://lotioncrafter.com/
- **Specialization**: Bulk skincare ingredients
- **Processing Time**: 1-3 business days
- **Special Features**: 
  - Wholesale pricing available
  - Multiple currency support (USD, INR, GBP, CAD, AUD, EUR, JPY)
  - Formulary and calculator tools

### 3. From Nature With Love (FNWL)
- **Website**: https://www.fromnaturewithlove.com/
- **Specialization**: Organic & natural personal care ingredients
- **Founded**: 1997
- **Certifications**: 
  - Organic Trade Association member
  - California Certified Organic Farmers (CCOF) certified
- **Special Features**: 
  - FNWL Rewards program (10% of merchandise subtotal)
  - Professional artisan focus
  - Extensive organic ingredient line

## Supplier Network Categories

### 1. Premium Actives Suppliers
- Focus on high-concentration active ingredients
- Price range: $50-$800 per kg
- Target: Professional formulators and brands

### 2. Bulk Base Suppliers
- Focus on foundational ingredients
- Price range: $5-$50 per kg
- Target: Large-scale manufacturers

### 3. Organic/Natural Specialists
- Focus on certified organic and natural ingredients
- Premium pricing for certification
- Target: Natural/organic brands

### 4. Specialty Chemical Suppliers
- Focus on novel and patented ingredients
- Highest price tier
- Target: Innovation-focused brands

## Market Trends Identified

1. **Increasing demand for organic certification**
2. **Growing interest in fermented and bioactive ingredients**
3. **Supply chain challenges affecting specialty ingredients**
4. **Bulk pricing advantages for quantities >10kg**
5. **Multi-currency support indicating global market**
