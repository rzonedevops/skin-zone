## Company Profile

Making Cosmetics is a wholesale cosmetic ingredients supplier based in Washington, USA, specializing in natural and high-quality ingredients. The company operates an ISO/GMP certified and FDA registered repackaging facility, offering both retail and bulk sizes at discount rates.

### Certifications & Standards
- **ISO/GMP Certified** repackaging facility
- **FDA Registered** facility
- **USDA Organic Certified** ingredients available
- **OTC (Over-the-Counter) Drugs** including sunscreens, antiperspirants, and anti-acne agents

### Product Categories
- **Total Ingredients**: 1,124+ items
- **Packaging Options**: Retail sizes and large bulk quantities
- **Specialty Categories**: Organic, OTC drugs, cosmeceuticals, sensitive skin actives, skin tone actives
